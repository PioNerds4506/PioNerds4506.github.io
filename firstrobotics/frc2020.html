2020 FRC Season

Infinite Recharge

Infinite Recharge is the game for the 2020 FIRST Robotics Competition. The game is played by two alliances of three teams each. Teams work to protect FIRST city from asteroids by activating the Shield Generator. To do this, they must score power cells (foam balls) and manipulate control panels. At the end of the match, robots can climb onto the generator switch to make the shield generator operational.
